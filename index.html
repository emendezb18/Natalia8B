Korean War(1945-1953)

Before war North Korea

  • Landforms: Rugged mountainous terrain, including the Baekdu Mountain.
  • Bodies of Water: East Sea (Sea of Japan) to the east.
  • Cities: Pyongyang (capital), Hamhung, Chongjin.


    • Before war South Korea

  • Landforms: Varied terrain with mountainous regions and plains.
  • Bodies of Water: Yellow Sea to the west, East China Sea to the south.
  • Cities: Seoul (capital), Busan, Incheon.




    • After war North Korea

  • Landforms: Similar rugged terrain.
  • Bodies of Water: Borders the East Sea.
  • Cities: Pyongyang remains as ssssthe capital .


    • North Korea adopts a socialist regime under Kim Il-sung's leadership, while South Korea evolves into a democratic state.


    After war South Korea

  • Landforms: Similar varied terrain.
  • Bodies of Water: Borders the Yellow Sea and the East China Sea.
  • Cities: Seoul remains as the capital.


    • South Korea grows economically.
